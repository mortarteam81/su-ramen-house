# Wave 15 — Elementary-Friendly Pacing Balance

## Goal
Lower overall difficulty for elementary-school players, especially on mobile.

## Changes
- Increased base patience for all customer types.
- Slowed customer spawn intervals across easy/normal/challenge presets.
- Reduced daily goal counts:
  - Day 1: 5 bowls
  - Day 2: 7 bowls
  - Day 3: 9 bowls
  - Day 4: 10 bowls
  - Day 5: 12 bowls
- Day 1 and Day 2 now use `easy` difficulty.
- Day 4 uses `normal`; Day 5 remains `challenge` but with softer challenge values.
- Mobile pacing adds:
  - +35% spawn interval
  - +18% patience
  - extra early-customer breather for first few customers

## Rationale
Mobile input is slower than desktop mouse input, and the target audience is elementary-school players. The game should reward learning and experimentation before applying time pressure.
